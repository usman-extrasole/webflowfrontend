Frequently asked questions

Some initial answers to get your started

I don’t know anything about technology. Is that okay?

Absolutely! We founded Stellant on the idea of making sure technology solutions were accessible to everyone. We can help you on your journey from wherever you're starting from.

How long does it take to get started?

We understand how time critical technology projects can be and can be ready to support projects in under 24 hours depending on the type of work and scope.

I’ve never worked with an offshore team. Is it safe?

Absolutely. We're set up to work in secure environments and also firewall all projects, so that only key team members can access information and files.

I just have an idea. Is that enough?

Yes. We can work with you to build out your idea or take a more fully flushed idea and turn it into a working prototype or full product.

How much does it cost?

Cost will depend on the type of engagement and the scope of the project. But given our ability to tap global tech talent, you may see up to 70% reductions in cost.
